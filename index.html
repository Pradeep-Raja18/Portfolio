About Me

I am a Fashion Designer & Stylist Specialised in Wedding Couture Wear

I am a highly curious and driven individual with a strong passion for exploration, creativity, and problem-solving-never afraid of challenges andnever willing to give up. As a Fashion and Luxury Marketing student seeking a six-month internship, I aim to transform my creativity into measurable value, delivering refined results and genuine client satisfaction.

With over three years of experience as a Fashion Designer and Stylist for high-end clientele, I bring hands-on expertise in couture design, bespoke fittings, and premium client service.

My background working on wedding and exclusive occasion wear has strengthened my eye for detail, quality control, and brand-aligned aesthetics. I combine strong client management skills with a deep understanding of luxury expectations, making me ready to contribute to retail excellence, client relations, production, or styling teams.
